Unicorn Gundam symbol

A symbol representing the RX-0 Unicorn Gundam and the company that built the mobile suit during the event's of Universal Century 0096, 16 years after the event's of the one year war, and 3 years after Char's counter attack.

Noble Team symbol

The symbol used for noble Team, a highly trained group of spartans, usually small, to complete certain tasks assigned to them. This team was assigned to Reach, a world that was important to humanity and tasked with defending it from the covenant.
